Youth Rights & Reforms

We believe young people deserve real power — not symbolism.

Our Core Reforms

  • Voting at 13 — with understanding checks, not social pressure
  • Truth Access — social media must prioritize fact over algorithm
  • Child Safety Support — for high-risk children, not punishment
  • Adoption-Only Stay — no automatic citizenship after irregular arrival
  • Co-Govs With Power — when we govern, local groups get real authority

No Misunderstandings

We do not support:

  • Locking children in padded rooms
  • Forcing clothing or diapers
  • Punishing families without offering help first

Our goal is **protection with dignity** — not control.
