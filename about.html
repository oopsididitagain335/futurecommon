About FutureCommon

FutureCommon is not a protest. It’s a prototype for the government of the future.

Our Mission

We are building a democracy that:

  • Starts at 13 — with verified youth voting
  • Is self-correcting — leaders can be removed if they betray trust
  • Protects the most vulnerable — with medical-grade safety for high-risk children
  • Is transparent — every promise tracked, every decision recorded

Our Timeline

This is not a rebellion. It’s a roadmap:

  • Now: We run co-gov pilots, test ideas, grow our team.
  • When we gain power: Co-govs get real authority. Reforms become law.
  • At 18: I will launch FutureCommon as a registered political party.

We are not asking for permission. We are building the future — and inviting you in.
