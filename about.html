About FutureCommon

FutureCommon is not a protest. It is a prototype for the government of the future — built by youth, for the next generation.

Our Mission

We are building a democracy that is:

  • Self-Correcting — leaders who betray trust are removed
  • Youth-Powered — voting at 13, co-govs at 16
  • Preventive — we stop harm before it happens
  • Truth-First — no algorithmic lies, no spin

Our Timeline

This is not a rebellion. It is a roadmap:

  • Now: We run co-gov pilots, test ideas, grow our team.
  • When we gain power: Co-govs get real budgets and authority.
  • At 18: I will launch FutureCommon as a registered political party.

We are not asking for permission. We are building the future — and inviting you in.
