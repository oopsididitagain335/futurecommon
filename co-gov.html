Co-Governance: A Pilot for the Future

Right now, we’re testing co-govs — but they have no legal power.

What Is a Co-Gov?

A local group of young people solving real problems — like:

  • Fixing a broken playground
  • Designing a mental health peer circle
  • Deciding how youth funds are spent

But Real Power Comes Later

These are **demos**, not government — yet.

When FutureCommon gains political power, co-govs will:

  • Have real budgets
  • Make binding decisions (with majority support)
  • Be legally recognized

We’re not bypassing democracy. We’re rehearsing it — until we’re ready to lead.
