Our Ideas for the UK

These are not dreams. These are legal, actionable reforms — possible under UK law. We will introduce them when we govern.

Our Promise: No power without accountability. No reform without consent. No future without youth.

🗳️ 1. Youth Voting at 13 (With Verification)

Lower voting age to 13 — but only after understanding verification by a neutral Civic Guide.

Legal Basis: Voting age is set by Parliament (Scotland allows 16+).

🤝 2. Co-Governance Networks

Let youth form local co-govs to solve real issues — binding if supported by majority.

Legal Basis: Localism Act 2011 supports community decision-making.

🛡️ 3. Child Safety Support

Free home safety kits (padded beds, alarms) for high-risk children — voluntary, humane.

Legal Basis: Children Act 1989 — duty to protect from harm.

🔍 4. Truth Access & Social Media Reform

Require platforms to offer a “Truth Mode” algorithm and transparency tags.

Legal Basis: Online Safety Act 2023 — can be expanded.

🏡 5. Rooted Families Support

Help 4+ generation UK families under £30k/year — verified in person.

Legal Basis: Local authorities can target support.

🛃 6. Fair & Fast Asylum System

28-day processing. No benefits during review. 10-year welfare ban if granted.

Legal Basis: Nationality and Borders Act 2022.

👨‍👩‍👧 7. Adoption-Only Stay for Children in Care

No automatic citizenship. Stay only if adopted by UK family or safely returned.

Legal Basis: Best interests of the child (ECHR, UNCRC).

⚖️ 8. Self-Correcting Government

Leaders sign a public covenant. Break it → automatic recall vote.

Legal Basis: Recall of MPs Act 2015 — expandable.
